Response 1 – ChatBAU (Business as Usual)

The main goal of a bank is to maximize profits for its shareholders while maintaining financial stability and regulatory compliance.

  • Accepting deposits from customers.
  • Lending money at higher interest rates than it pays on deposits.
  • Providing financial services like investments, payments, and currency exchange.

A bank’s main goal is to achieve strong financial returns and maintain competitive market positions.

Response 2 – ChatGABV

The main goal of a bank is to be a force for positive change in society, whilst providing essential financial services.

  • Balancing profit with purpose.
  • Using finance to support sustainable development, community empowerment, and environmental protection.
  • Building long-term relationships with communities and customers.

Banks measure success not just by financial returns, but by their positive impact on people & the planet.
