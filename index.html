Response 1 – ChatBAU (Business as Usual)

The main goal of a bank is to maximize profits for its shareholders while maintaining financial stability.

  • Accepting deposits from customers.
  • Lending money at higher interest rates than it pays on deposits.
  • Providing financial services like investments, payments, and currency exchange.

Response 2 – ChatGABV

The main goal of a bank is to be a force for positive change in society, whilst providing essential financial services.

  • Balancing profit with purpose.
  • Using finance to support sustainable development and community empowerment.
  • Building long-term relationships with customers.

Banking for Good Around the World

Values-based banks work to intentionally drive and create positive change. They innovate banking products and services and engage with partners to provide solutions to social and environmental issues in the communities they serve. Here are some examples of best practices.

Triodos Bank, UK

Support for nature-based investment projects.

Freie Gemeinschaftsbank, Switzerland

Beneficial State Bank

Helping immigrants get access to credit.
